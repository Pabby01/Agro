General Questions

What is AgroPlus?

AgroPlus is an agricultural fintech platform that connects farmers with buyers and provides financial services. We offer a marketplace for agricultural products, loans for farmers, and various financial solutions to support agricultural businesses.

How do I sell my products on AgroPlus?

To sell on AgroPlus, register as a seller, verify your account, and list your agricultural products. Our team will review your listings to ensure quality standards before they go live.

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer various agricultural loans including equipment financing, working capital loans, and land acquisition loans. Interest rates start from 3%, with flexible repayment terms up to 5 years.

Marketplace

How is product quality assured?

All products listed on AgroPlus undergo quality verification. We work with certified agricultural inspectors to ensure products meet industry standards before they are listed.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, Paystack, Flutterwave, and other secure payment options. All transactions are protected by our secure payment system.

Financial Services

What are the loan requirements?

Basic requirements include being a registered farmer or agricultural business, minimum 2 years of farming experience, valid ID, and bank statements. Additional requirements vary by loan type.

How long does loan approval take?

Loan applications are typically processed within 48 hours. Once approved, funds are disbursed within 3-5 business days.
